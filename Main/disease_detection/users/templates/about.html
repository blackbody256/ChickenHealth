About KukuIntel

We're on a mission to revolutionize poultry farming through artificial intelligence, empowering farmers worldwide with the tools they need to maintain healthier flocks and build sustainable futures.

Our Mission

At KukuIntel, we believe that technology should serve humanity's most fundamental need: food security. Our mission is to empower poultry farmers, vets, and agricultural professionals with AI-driven tools that make flock management more efficient, sustainable, and profitable.

We're building a future where every farmer, regardless of their location or resources, has access to world-class poultry intelligence that helps them make informed decisions about their flocks.
